Overview

Purpose

To provide a platform where we can be able to further market our rafting company and increase it its reach. This will inherently increase revenue and in turn will help us become a more successful company with more revenue and sucess. We also want to inform our clients about what to bring, costs, dangers, location, etc. This site will be able to provide all of this which will allow us to come accross as a reputable rafting company.

Audience

Our target audience is large groups like families, church groups, scount groups, etc. We clients that bring a large group of people so that we can maximize time spent on the water. We want our clients to be suffiently well off to the point where they are willing to pay for extra equipment and provide generous tips to our employees. They should want to have a good time and ennjoy being on the water so that we are able to establish return customers. The needs that arent being met are proper information, prices, etc. which is why having a sight is important. They will access it via desktop and mobile devices.
